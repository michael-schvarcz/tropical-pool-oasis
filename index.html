Bluetooth Speaker Setup

Connect to our VOCBOOM speaker in a few simple steps:

1

Power On

Long press the Power button (~3 seconds) until you hear a startup chime or see the LED begin to flash.

2

Enter Pairing Mode

Long press the Play/Pause button to activate Bluetooth pairing.

3

Search on Your Device

Open Bluetooth settings on your phone, tablet, or computer and scan for new devices.

4

Select the Speaker

Look for "VOCBOOM" in the list and tap to connect.

5

Confirmation

You'll hear a beep and the LED indicator will turn solid blue. You're all set!
