Bluetooth Speaker

›

Thirty seconds and you're the DJ — Bluetooth pairing has to be done in your phone's settings (no way around that on any phone), but it's only two steps:

  1. Wake the speaker: hold its power button until you hear the chime.
  2. On your phone: Settings → Bluetooth → tap Oasis Speaker. Done — press play.

It stays paired for your whole visit, and anyone in your group can take a turn.

Please keep music at a respectful volume for our neighbors. 🌴
